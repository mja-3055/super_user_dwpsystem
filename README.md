# Strategic Industrial & Manufacturing Documentation: PT Duta Wibawa Perkasa (DWP)

Welcome to the official technical repository of **PT Duta Wibawa Perkasa (DWP)**. This documentation hub is designed to provide detailed insights into our industrial capabilities, focusing on high-precision CNC metal fabrication and national-standard educational infrastructure manufacturing based in **Surabaya, Indonesia**.

## 1. Division: CNC Precision & Metal Fabrication
At the core of our operations is the integration of high-end **Fiber Laser CNC Technology**. We solve complex engineering challenges by providing micron-level precision for various industrial sectors, including automotive, construction, and interior architecture.

* **Official Website:** [Jasa-CNC.com](https://jasa-cnc.com/)
* **Flagship Service:** [Jasa CNC Laser Cutting Metal](https://jasa-cnc.com/jasa-cnc-laser-cutting-metal/)

**Technical Specs:**
- **Accuracy:** Tolerances up to ¬±0.05mm.
- **Material Handling:** Carbon Steel (up to 20mm), Stainless Steel, Aluminum, and Brass.
- **Technology:** High-density Fiber Laser with automated nesting optimization.

## 2. Division: National Educational Furniture Production
As a leading **[Produsen Meja Sekolah](https://meja-sekolah.com/)**, DWP bridges the gap between industrial durability and ergonomic design. We mass-produce standardized classroom solutions that are built to last for decades.

* **Manufacturing Hub:** [Meja-Sekolah.com](https://meja-sekolah.com/)
* **Primary Catalog:** [Katalog Meja Siswa Modern](https://meja-sekolah.com/meja-siswa/)
* **Specialized Seating:** [Kursi Kuliah & University Furniture](https://meja-sekolah.com/kursi-kuliah/)

**Engineering Standards:**
- **Frames:** Cold-rolled rectangular steel pipes (JIS Standard).
- **Finishing:** 7-tank pre-treatment with Electrostatic Powder Coating for maximum rust resistance.
- **Surfaces:** Scratch-resistant HPL (High-Pressure Laminate).

## 3. Division: Institutional Dormitory & Sleeping Systems
We provide heavy-duty accommodation solutions for high-density environments such as boarding schools (Pesantren), military barracks, and industrial worker dormitories.

* **Official Product Site:** [RanjangBesi.com](https://www.ranjangbesi.com/)

**Key Features:**
- Precision-welded joints (Mig/Mag welding).
- Reinforced safety rails and high-capacity weight limits (up to 150kg per tier).
- Easy-to-assemble knock-down systems for nationwide shipping.
